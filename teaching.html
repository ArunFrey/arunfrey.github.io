Introduction to R

I'll be teaching the Intro to R course at Oxford this Michelmas term (2022).

The course is designed to acquaint students with the basic principles behind R. Students will learn how to read, write, and understand code, load and manipulate data, deal with missing values, and explore fun datasets.

All material is taught in base R.

You can find all course information, including slides, homework, and exercises, on my Github repository. To preview the HTML slides, I suggest you follow this link.

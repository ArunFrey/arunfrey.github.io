Hi there!

My name is Arun Frey. I'm a social data scientist working towards improving urgent social problems through data and social insights. Currently, I'm a postdoctoral researcher at the Leverhulme Centre for Demographic Science, University of Oxford and a Data Science for Social Good Fellow at Carnegie Mellon University. In my research I develop computational methods for the study of immigration, integration, and inequality. My work has been covered by the New York Times, the Economist, The BBC, and other outlets, and was recently awarded with the National Academy of Sciences Cozzarelli Prize.

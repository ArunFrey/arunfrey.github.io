Hi there!

My name is Arun Frey. I'm a data scientist and sociologist, and I care about leveraging data for social impact. Currently, I'm a data scientist at The Regulation, Evaluation, and Governance Lab (RegLab) at Stanford University, where I work on developing machine learning solutions to modernise government processes. Prior to that, I was a postdoctoral researcher at the Leverhulme Centre for Demographic Science, University of Oxford. In my academic research I develop computational methods for the study of migration and inequality. My work has been covered by the New York Times, the Economist, The BBC, and other outlets, and was recently awarded with the National Academy of Sciences Cozzarelli Prize.

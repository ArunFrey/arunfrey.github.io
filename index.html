Hi there!

My name is Arun Frey. I'm a sociologist and data scientist. Currently, I'm a postdoctoral researcher at the Leverhulme Centre for Demographic Science, University of Oxford and a Data Science for Social Good Fellow at Carnegie Mellon University. I'm particularly interested in leveraging data for social impact. In my research I develop computational methods for the study of migration, conflict, and inequality. My work has been covered by the New York Times, the Economist, The BBC, and other outlets, and was recently awarded with the National Academy of Sciences Cozzarelli Prize.
